About Rocket Logistic

Your reliable partner in logistics. We are a company with great capabilities and a personal approach, ready to offer flexible and fast solutions for every transport need. Based in Targovishte in Northeast Bulgaria, we serve clients across the Black Sea region including Burgas, Varna, Shumen, and Razgrad, with connections to Sofia and international routes.

In our pursuit of modern and sustainable logistics, we have implemented fully digital document management. Through online data exchange, we not only optimize workflows and reduce the final cost of transport for our clients, but also actively limit our carbon footprint. By choosing us, you get efficiency, cost savings, and a responsible attitude towards nature.
